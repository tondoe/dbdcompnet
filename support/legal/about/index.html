About us

Welcome to the DBDCompNet, or in the longer version "DBD Competitive Network".

The DBDCompNet offers centralized all-system solutions "all things comp Dead by Daylight" for all organizations, communities and content creators of Dead by Daylight.

Our motto is "together into the next generation", an idea after "Into The Fog" and "Comp Reimagined".

History

The DBDCompNet was first ideally created on the 21st of July 2024 by the DBDMCLeague Lead-Commission (sxmexnedbd), formerly this project was made to be exclusively for Dead by Daylight Mobile. Later in 2025, when the announcement was made that Dead by Daylight Mobile would shut-down, this idea was cancelled.

In late 2025, the Lead-Commission of DBDCLeague (sxmexnedbd) made the decision to continue this plan of a centralized platform "all things comp Dead by Daylight". In November 2025 the DBDCompNet was made an official product by the DBD Comp Union.

Now, in 2026, the DBD Comp Union's Lead-Commission (sxmexnedbd) separated the DBDCompNet from the DBD Comp Union: To open doors for more than just members of an alliance. The DBDCompNet Management Team is now responsible for the DBDCompNet platform and all its features. The project is still under the lead of the DBDCompNet Administration (sxmexnedbd).

Service

The DBDCompNet offers endless functions: Endless because we still develop this platform with ideas from organizations and players.

To help administration be made easier, but still keep the control within the organizations, we offer the teams and players to organize and administer themselves within all organizations globally, centralized in one place. Via the DBDCompNet Team Management (MyTeam) the captain can add new players, remove players, change team critical information like name and region and give away his ownership (captain status) to another. Teams can join / apply to join tournaments and oversee general rules, conducts and guidelines of all organizations.

Organizations may use the DBDCompNet as their own (in accordance with the Terms of Service) and manage teams: Suspend, unsuspend, allow and deny to their organization, create and manage events, give permissions and more.

Mission

The DBDCompNet is developed to function like a central institution. It serves the purpose to oversee organizations, protect and ensure a safe environment between players and organizations, and also build trust between organizations.

We organize our platform to fit with all organizations: fix administrative burnouts, make scheduling easy, build trust between orgs, offer standard solutions, lift production quality, and solve continuous issues raised by organizations.

Fix Administrative Burnout

Administrative people may manage players manually, collect fees manually, and do a lot of manual work for staff management.

The solution is to let players manage their own teams through this service while automated systems and bots issue those changes across organizations without the same manual workload. Fee collection and similar admin processes can also be integrated into the platform later.

Scheduling Easy (EasySchedule)

Scheduling is a major problem when players or teams cannot make tournament times or sign up and then do not show up.

The solution is to offer available-time displays for teams, event start and end estimates, and systems that can prevent teams from joining events that do not fit their schedule. The platform can also support separate scheduling and streaming solutions for out-of-schedule teams.

Build Trust Between Orgs

Organizations often have trust issues with other organizations and do not always know how safe, secure, or trustworthy another group may be.

The solution is communication. With the forums, we want to offer the option for organizations to publicly communicate regulations, balancing, and possibly build alliances.

Offer Standard Solutions

Balancing disputes, too many different balancing options, and disputes between organizations or players can all create more administrative burnout.

The solution is to offer standard solutions through DBDCompNet balancing, central rules, dispute resolution systems, and report systems that help organizations and players find practical outcomes.

Lift Production Quality

Events are often hosted months in advance so organizers can create backgrounds, graphics, and other material, which can leave long periods where players have less to do.

The platform helps fill those gaps by making events easier to find, supporting weekly scrims, and offering more ongoing competitive activity while organizers focus on their larger productions.

Other Continuous Problems

Some organizations do not openly communicate their problems or do not have a direct partner working on solutions.

The solution is simple: tell us your problems and issues with competitive Dead by Daylight and we will work on exactly that solution.

It also serves players as a platform to manage teams, add and remove players, change team names, change captains, participate in events, and overview teams and players, including communication.

It serves organizations as a platform to manage teams, suspend players or teams, allow or deny teams in tournaments, manage events, set regulations, and overview teams and players, including communication.

Vision

We visionize a project that will continue over the years and remain active: a centralized solution for both the big and the small.

Organizations can speak directly to a partner for services and concerns and save time with manual work and workloads, while we continue working on automation for those processes.

We start this project in November 2024 with first visual representation in November 2025 and first development with the ideas in January 2026.

We have developed a plan for a variety of projects like our Balancing Service, Ranked DBD, DBD Comp Network Awards, Hub for default players and content creators, Divisions including Top Division Championships, and Rating Systems to work more toward trust and safety.
