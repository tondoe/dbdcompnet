The DBD Comp Network Code of Conduct is the foundation for organizations, communities, leagues, and similar clients operating inside the DBDCompNet structure.

I. - Introduction & Scope

This code establishes expectations for current and future client organizations and supplements the general Terms of Service.

II. - Appearance & Values

Organizations are expected to act legally, remain organized, and present themselves in a respectful, fair, and low-controversy way while respecting Behaviour Interactive's rules and broader community standards.

III. - (Client) Organization Definition

An organization is treated as a group of at least two natural persons operating a community presence, primarily through Discord and at least one public-facing platform outside Discord.

IV. - Extern Representation

Organizations using the platform represent DBDCompNet externally and are expected to avoid conduct that damages trust, safety, or platform reputation.

Information I. - Organizations with Official Recognition

Official recognition by Behaviour Interactive or Dead by Daylight does not automatically guarantee approval into DBDCompNet.

Information II. - Unable to Check Your Organization

If staff cannot reasonably verify an applicant organization, the application may be rejected and future applications may be delayed.

Information III. - Ending the Client Relationship

Client relationship termination follows the relevant sections of the general Terms of Service.

Information IV. - Custom Agreement

Custom agreements may exist at DBDCompNet's discretion, but they may be declined and can include indemnification or liability-related clauses where appropriate.
