Community Standards

Back to Home

The DBDCompNet Community Standards define expected behavior for all users on the platform. These standards are enforced to maintain a safe, fair, and competitive environment.

1. Respectful Conduct

Users must treat others with respect. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or abusive behavior is strictly prohibited.

2. Fair Competition

All players must follow tournament rules and maintain competitive integrity. Cheating, exploiting, or manipulating systems is not allowed.

3. Account Responsibility

Users are responsible for their accounts. Sharing accounts with third parties is not permitted.

4. Content Guidelines

Content shared on the platform must not include illegal, harmful, or offensive material.

5. Enforcement

Violations of these standards may result in warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans depending on severity.
