Welcome to the Community Standards of DBDCompNet. These standards explain how users are expected to behave across the website, Discord-connected spaces, support systems, events, and related services.

I. - Scope and Services

This policy applies to user conduct across DBDCompNet spaces and supplements the general Terms of Service.

II. - Safety

Content or behavior involving child exploitation, terrorism, violent extremism, targeted abuse, suicide encouragement, serious threats, harassment, doxxing, or other severe safety risks is prohibited.

III. - Off-Limits

DBDCompNet may prohibit illegal content, explicit abuse, impersonation, hate-driven conduct, fraud, malicious links, exploit trading, and other material that threatens users, the platform, or the community.

IV. - Integrity

Users are expected to act honestly, avoid deceptive conduct, and not misuse platform systems, moderation channels, or other users for personal advantage.

V. - Advertisement

Advertising, promotion, and recruitment must respect platform rules, staff direction, and any additional restrictions applied to community spaces or event systems.

VI. - Security

Users may not attempt to bypass protections, attack the service, leak sensitive information, or exploit security weaknesses for gain or disruption.

VII. - Misusing Automatic Safety & Security Enforcements

Attempts to trigger, abuse, or manipulate moderation, reporting, or automated security systems may result in warnings, suspensions, or removal.
