Welcome to the DBDCompNet Privacy Policy. This document explains how the platform collects, uses, stores, protects, and discloses personal data connected to accounts and related services.

I. - Scope and Services

This policy applies to users of DBDCompNet websites, applications, account systems, support services, social channels, and affiliated services unless a separate affiliate policy is provided.

II. - Data Collection

Collected data may include contact, identity, profile, employment or application data, content data, technical data, usage data, and communication preferences. DBDCompNet does not intentionally collect special-category personal data unless explicitly required and lawfully provided.

III. - How We Use Your Data

Personal data may be processed to operate accounts, manage relationships with users, review applications, run services, provide support, maintain security, comply with law, and deliver communications where permitted.

IV. - How We Collect Your Data

Data may be collected through direct user actions, public submissions, support requests, account creation, organization and team registration, analytics tools, cookies, and service interactions.

V. - Disclosure of Your Personal Data

DBDCompNet may share data with service providers, organization partners, technical processors, and authorities where required by law, platform security, fraud prevention, or operational necessity.

VI. - Location of Your Personal Data

Data may be processed or stored in locations relevant to the service and the providers used to operate it, subject to applicable safeguards and lawful transfer practices.

VII. - How Long We Keep Your Personal Data

Data is retained only as long as needed for platform operation, legal compliance, dispute handling, fraud prevention, account history, and related service purposes.

VIII. - How We Keep Your Personal Data Safe

DBDCompNet uses technical and organizational measures intended to reduce unauthorized access, misuse, disclosure, or loss of personal data.

IX. - Your Rights Regarding Your Personal Data

Users may request access, correction, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, or consent withdrawal where applicable under GDPR and similar rules.

X. - Information Regarding Minors

Where age-based restrictions apply, DBDCompNet may restrict, suspend, or remove access that does not comply with platform or legal requirements.

XI. - Third-Party Links

Third-party sites, providers, or integrations may have their own privacy practices. Users should review those policies separately.

XII. - Marketing

Marketing communications are only sent where permitted, requested, or consented to, and users may opt out or object where applicable.

XIII. - Changes to Our Privacy Policy

Policy changes may be published on the website, shown in-service, or announced through official channels.

XIV. - Contact Us

Privacy requests and questions may be sent through the support section or by email to the DBDCompNet support or privacy contact listed by staff.
