DBDCompNet Legal

When using footage of our events (“Services”), we ask you to follow these guidelines (“Policy”) to ensure that our footage is used correctly.

1. Scope of Policy

This Policy applies across all platforms that allow the upload of media and audio. Every platform may introduce specific policies for DMCA, Copyright, Trademark and other protections. These are valid on those platforms. Law applies.

2. Copyright Material

2.1 Premium-LIVE-Events (PLE)

Our Premium-LIVE-Events (“PLE”) are protected under these guidelines.

Premium-LIVE-Events include:

  • DBD Comp Awards
  • Best of the Best

These events are reupload-able under the following conditions:

  • The original creator (DBD Comp Union) must be tagged in the description
  • A link to the DBD Comp Union website and Discord must be included
  • A visible banner crediting DBD Comp Union must be shown (video)
  • Audio must state: “original footage was taken from the DBD Comp Union”

If these conditions are followed, action will generally not be taken unless the content includes hateful speech or unacceptable topics.

2.2 Other Events

Other events, including smaller or co-hosted events, are generally free to reupload unless the main broadcaster objects.

However, action may still be taken if the content does not align with our standards.

2.3 Media

All original media used by DBD Comp Union that is not AI-generated is protected under copyright.

The Dead by Daylight logo used in media is owned by Behaviour Interactive.

Usage of media (photos, GIFs, etc.) is not allowed unless explicitly provided by a Lead Commission or Commission member.

2.4 Text Content

All written content including website text, rules, guidelines, and advertisements are strictly copyrighted and may not be used by third parties without permission.

3. Compilations

Content creators may use material under fair use. However, Twitch clips, YouTube Shorts, and TikToks are still considered copyrighted content.

When creating compilations, the following must be followed:

  • The DBD Comp Union website must be linked in the description
  • Official event banners must not be removed or altered
