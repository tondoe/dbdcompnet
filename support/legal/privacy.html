Welcome to the DBDCompNet Privacy Policy. This policy exists for the purpose of transparency and to explain how we process your information and work to protect your privacy.

1. Scope and Services

1.1 Scope of Policy

This applies when you (“user”) use our Services offered by the DBDCompNet Management or any of its affiliates or partners (referred to as “we”, “our”), unless an affiliate has published their own privacy policy.

The terms of this policy together with our Terms of Service apply to your use of the Services. In certain circumstances, we may provide additional privacy-related information. This policy supplements such other notices.

1.2 Definition of Services

This policy gives you information on how we collect, use, process and disclose Personal Data that you provide us through the usage of our websites, applications and your DBDCompNet Account, social media channels, user support, and other services that DBDCompNet provides, whether downloaded or streamed onto your device (the “Services”).

2. Data Collection

2.1 Type of Personal Data

We may collect, use, store and share different kinds of personal data about you, including:

  • Contact Data: email address.
  • Content Data: information stored on your device, including login information.
  • Employment Data: personal information provided in applications and staff-related messages.
  • Identity Data: legal names, usernames, or similar identifiers.
  • Marketing / Communication Data: communication and advertising preferences.
  • Profile Data: username, password, registered organizations, survey responses, support requests, and other shared information.
  • Technical Data: IP address, general location, login data, browser information, timezone, OS, platform, and device technology.
  • Usage Data: information, preferences, and statistics about how you use our Services.

We may also collect, use, store, and disclose aggregated data such as statistical or demographic data for any purpose. Aggregated Data is not considered Personal Data where it does not directly or indirectly reveal your identity.

We do not collect any special categories of Personal Data unless you provide your express consent, including:

  • race or ethnicity
  • religious, political, or philosophical beliefs
  • sexual orientation
  • health information

2.2 Failing to Provide Personal Data

Where we are required to collect Personal Data by law or under a contract with you, and you fail to provide that data when requested, we may not be able to perform the contract or provide certain Services or features.

3. Our Use of Your Data

3.1 Purpose for Which We Use Your Personal Data

We may use your Personal Data under the following GDPR legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): to manage your DBDCompNet account, administer the Services, provide support, process applications, enable surveys or contests, facilitate communication, and operate the DBDCompNet system across Discord server organizations.
  • Compliance with legal obligations (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR): to comply with law, regulation, legal process, investigations, and to address security issues or criminal activities.
  • Your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): to deliver relevant content, personalized advertisements, and enable participation in contests, draws, or surveys.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): for troubleshooting, fraud prevention, player bans, updates, testing, IT services, and network security.

3.2 Opting Out and Changing Purpose

You can ask us to stop sending you marketing and advertising messages at any time. Opting out of marketing communications will not affect Personal Data provided to us for service-related purposes.

3.3 Use of Your Data for Other Purposes

We only use your Personal Data for the purposes stated in this policy. If we need to use it for another purpose, we will notify you and explain the legal basis unless otherwise permitted or required by law.

4. How Your Personal Data Is Collected

4.1 Direct Interactions

We may receive information directly from you when you:

  • apply for Services, subscribe to newsletters, or register for Services
  • create an account or profile
  • enter a contest, tournament, or survey, or create user content
  • contact support, request technical help, give feedback, or apply for a position

4.2 Data Available Publicly

Personal Data you disclose in public areas of our Services, including forums, blogs, chats, discussion platforms, and public user profiles, may become public and may be read or used by others. You disclose such information at your own risk.

4.3 Automated Technologies, Cookies, Analytics Tools and Other Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, pixels, SDKs, log files, and analytics tools to collect technical and usage data such as IP address, device identifiers, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, access times, and interaction data.

Cookies may be session cookies or persistent cookies. We use them for core functionality, remembering preferences, improving performance, measuring engagement, and preventing fraud or misuse.

Where required by law, non-essential cookies are only used with your prior consent, which you may withdraw or modify through your cookie preferences or browser settings.

4.4 Third Parties

We may receive Personal Data from:

  • service providers, affiliates, vendors, and contractors
  • publicly available sources such as public posts and comments
  • business partners and clients (“Members”)
  • advertising partners
  • other third parties you have agreed may share data with us

5. Disclosure of Your Personal Data

5.1 Our Third Parties

Members of DBDCompNet across the globe may have access to your Personal Data where necessary to provide the Services they offer, in compliance with this Policy.

DBDCompNet Admins, including organizations with an Access Agreement (“non-Member”), may access your information similarly to Members.

5.2 External Third Parties

We do not share your Personal Data with external third parties unless:

  • Fraud and Legal: BHVR Inc. requests your data for legal reasons, fraud, business protection, or prevention of scams harming users.
  • Law Enforcement: authorities or affiliated persons request your data for lawful enforcement purposes.

In other cases, if someone requests access to your Personal Data without a lawful basis, we will generally deny the request or ask for your permission first.

6. Location of Your Personal Data

Personal Data may be hosted and processed on servers operated by trusted partners or service providers in Europe, such as IONOS SE.

If you are based in the EU, UK, Canada, or the US, your data may be transferred outside your jurisdiction where necessary. In such cases, we take steps to ensure an appropriate level of protection, including approved contractual safeguards where applicable.

7. Duration of Your Personal Data

We keep your Personal Data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this policy, including maintaining your DBDCompNet Account and meeting legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

We may anonymize your data for research or statistical purposes, in which case it may be used indefinitely. Otherwise, we will delete Personal Data when it is no longer required.

8. Safeguarding Your Personal Data

DBDCompNet Management takes measures to protect your Personal Data from abuse and misuse. Information is stored securely and protected through procedures and security features designed to prevent accidental loss, unauthorized access, or misuse.

If a Personal Data breach is detected, we will inform you and regulators where legally required. However, we are not responsible for the circumvention of privacy settings or security measures on our Services.

9. Your Rights

9.1 Your Legal Rights

Under data protection law, you may have the right to:

  • request access to your Personal Data
  • request correction of inaccurate data
  • request erasure of your Personal Data
  • object to processing of your Personal Data
  • request restriction of processing
  • request transfer of your Personal Data
  • withdraw consent at any time where consent is the basis of processing

You may also ask us not to continue processing your Personal Data for marketing purposes and may appeal adverse decisions related to your Personal Data.

9.2 Exercising Your Rights and Fees

We do not charge fees for exercising your legal rights. To protect your data, we may need to verify your identity before completing a request.

9.3 Additional Rights

Some jurisdictions may provide additional rights. To exercise those rights, contact us at support@dbdcompnet.com.

10. About Children

Our Services are not intended for children who are considered minors under the data protection law of their country of residence unless clearly allowed by a legal guardian.

We do not knowingly collect Personal Data from children without proper consent. If we learn that such data has been collected improperly, we will delete it.

11. Third-Party Links

Our Services may include links to third-party websites, plug-ins, or applications. Clicking those links may allow third parties to collect, use, or share your information. We do not control these third-party services and are not responsible for their privacy statements.

12. Direct Marketing Decision

We will only send direct marketing communications by email or text where we have your consent or where otherwise permitted by law. We will obtain your consent before sharing your Personal Data with third parties for marketing purposes, including personalized ads.

You may withdraw consent at any time or opt out of newsletters or promotional emails by following the instructions in those communications.

13. Changes to the Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy regularly. Changes will be posted on this page and may be highlighted for 7 days. You may also be notified by email or when logging into the Services.

If you do not accept updated terms where acceptance is required, your DBDCompNet Account may be terminated in accordance with the Terms of Service.
