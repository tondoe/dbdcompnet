1.1 Scope of Policy

This applies when you ("user") use our Services (defined below) offered by the DBDCompNet Management or any of its affiliates or partners (referred to as "we", "our"), unless an affiliate has published their own privacy policy.

The terms of this policy together with our Terms of Service applies to your use of the Services. In certain circumstances, we may provide additional privacy-related information. This policy supplements such other notices.

This policy gives you information on how we collect, use, process and disclose Personal Data that you provide us through the usage of our websites, applications and your DBDCompNet Account, social media channels and user support and other services that the DBDCompNet provides, which may be downloaded or streamed onto your device (the "Services").

2. Data Collection

2.1 Type of Personal Data

We may collect, use, store and share different kinds of personal data about you. This data contains following:

  • Contact Data: email address.
  • Content Data: includes information stored on your device, including login information.
  • Employment Data: includes any personal information that you provide or attach to application documents and messages for positions at the DBDCompNet Staff.
  • Identity Data: legal names, usernames or similar identifiers (identification codes of the platform you use).
  • Marketing/Communication Data: includes your preferences receiving marketing and advertisement from us and affiliated parties and how we can communicate with you.
  • Profile Data: includes your username, password, registered organizations, survey responses containing personal data, information you provide when contacting our support services, including other information that you share with us by using our Services.
  • Technical Data: includes internet protocol (IP) address, your general (but not precise) location, login data, browser and plug-in information, time zone, operating system and platform, and other technology on the devices you use to access our Services.
  • Usage Data: includes information, preferences and statistics about how you use our Services.

(all collectively referred to as "Personal Data").

We may also collect, use, store, and disclose Aggregated Data such as statistical or demographic data for any purpose. Aggregated Data could be derived from your Personal Data but is not considered Personal Data as this data will not (directly or indirectly) reveal your identity.

We do not collect any Specific / Special Categories of Personal Data about you (including details as follows):

  • race or ethnicity,
  • religious, political or philosophical beliefs,
  • sexual orientation,
  • health information,

unless you provide your express consent.

Below is a description of how we may use your Personal data (GDPR Art. 12).

We use automated technologies and tools to collect and process information about your use of our Services. These technologies help us operate, maintain, improve, and secure our Services, as well as understand how users interact with them.

Where required by applicable data protection and ePrivacy laws, we use non-essential cookies and similar technologies only with your prior consent. You may withdraw or modify your consent at any time through your cookie preferences or browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our Services.

Processing carried out through analytics and metrics tools is based on our legitimate interests in understanding and improving our Services (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR), unless consent is required under applicable law, in which case processing is based on Article 6(1)(a) GDPR.

9. Your Rights

9.1 Your Legal Rights

By law, about data protection, you have the following legal rights:

Request access to your Personal Data: You have the right to request confirmation as to whether we process your personal data and, if so, to obtain access to that data and related information.

Request correction of your Personal Data: You have the right to request the correction of inaccurate personal data and the completion of incomplete personal data we hold about you.

Request erasure of your Personal Data: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data where there is no lawful reason for us to continue processing it.

Object to processing of your Personal Data: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data where such processing is based on our legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes.

Request restriction of processing your Personal Data: You have the right to request that we limit the processing of your personal data under certain circumstances, such as while we verify its accuracy or assess an objection.

Request transfer of your Personal Data: You have the right to request the receipt or transfer of personal data you have provided to us in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

Right to withdraw consent: You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time where we rely on your consent to process your personal data, without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

You also have the right to ask us to not continue using and processing your data for marketing purposes, and to appeal any adverse decisions made by us regarding the processing of your Personal Data. In the event your consent was expressly provided to the DBD Comp Union or its members for ads personalization in our management Services ("DBD Competitive Network") you may withdraw your consent as indicated in the related Services.
