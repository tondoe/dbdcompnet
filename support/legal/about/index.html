About us

Welcome to the DBDCompNet, or in the longer version "DBD Competitive Network".

The DBDCompNet offers centralized all-system solutions "all things comp Dead by Daylight" for all organizations, communities and content creators of Dead by Daylight.

Our motto is "together into the next generation", an idea after "Into The Fog".

History

The DBDCompNet was first ideally created on the 21st of July 2024 by the DBDMCLeague Lead-Commission (sxmexnedbd), formerly this project was made to be exclusively for Dead by Daylight Mobile. Later in 2025, when the announcement was made that Dead by Daylight Mobile would shut-down, this idea was cancelled.

In late 2025, the Lead-Commission of DBDCLeague (sxmexnedbd) made the decision to continue this plan of a centralized platform "all things comp Dead by Daylight". In November 2025 the DBDCompNet was made an official product by the DBD Comp Union.

Now, in 2026, the DBD Comp Union's Lead-Commission (sxmexnedbd) separated the DBDCompNet from the DBD Comp Union: To open doors for more than just members of an alliance. The DBDCompNet Management Team is now responsible for the DBDCompNet platform and all its features. The project is still led under the DBDCompNet Administration (sxmexnedbd).

Service

The DBDCompNet offers endless functions: Endless because we still develop this platform with ideas from organizations and players.

To help administration be made easier, but still keep the control within the organizations, we offer the teams and players to organize and administer themselves within all organizations globally, centralized in one place. Via the DBDCompNet Team Management (MyTeam) the captain can add new players, remove players, change team critical information like name and region and give away his ownership (captain status) to another. Teams can join / apply to join tournaments and oversee general rules, conducts and guidelines of all organizations.

Organizations may use the DBDCompNet as their own (in accordance with the Terms of Service) and manage teams: Suspend, unsuspend, allow and deny to their organization, create and manage events, give permissions and more.
