Welcome to the Community Standards (“Policy”) of DBDCompNet. This Policy exists to set standards for communication and interaction between users of our Services.

Last updated: 19.05.2025

Active since: 01.01.2026

1. Scope and Services

1.1 Scope of Policy

This Policy applies when you (“user”) use our Services offered by DBDCompNet or any of its affiliates or partners (referred to as “we”, “our”), unless an affiliate has published their own community policy.

The terms of this Policy together with our Terms of Service apply to your use of the Services. In certain circumstances, we may provide additional privacy-related information. This Policy supplements such other notices.

1.2 Definition of Services

This Policy gives you information on our Community Standards and communication between users of DBDCompNet, our Discord server, email, support, tournaments, DBD Comp Awards, and streaming and video channels (“Services”).

2. Safety

We want our community to stay safe and protected. The following guidelines help enforce safety for all users.

2.1 Children

Any form of abuse toward children on any of our Services is strictly prohibited. Children are all individuals under the age of 18. Abuse toward children includes, but is not limited to:

  • Explaining explicit fantasies about children or actual events in which children are exploited
  • Disclosing inappropriate events with children or planning for such acts to be carried out
  • Asking children for any form of personal information or sharing such information with others
  • Other predatory behaviour
  • Threatening children in any form, especially to do something inappropriate
  • Sharing media or other documentation about children, including face-censored media
  • Talking to children in a suggestive way or making children talk in a suggestive way

Children who intentionally cause others to break these rules will be handled as prescribed in our Terms. Children under the age of 13 on Discord will be banned. Children under the age of 16 on DBDCompNet will be suspended even if prior consent by their legal guardian was provided.

2.2 Terrorism and Extremism

Terroristic behaviour, including threatening others and causing an extremist or terroristic environment, is forbidden. We have a zero-tolerance policy for content or behaviour that incites, condones, supports, glorifies, or promotes any terrorist or extremist organization or individual, foreign or domestic, including:

  • Depictions of or support for terrorist or extremist attacks
  • Depictions of or support for the leaders or representatives of terrorist or extremist organizations
  • Sharing slogans, images, flags, manifestos, or icons of terrorist or extremist organizations
  • References to the ideologies, messages, or strategies of terrorist and extremist organizations
  • Expressing support for, condoning, or glorifying terrorist or extremist ideologies or actions
  • Recruiting membership for terrorist or extremist organizations
  • Fundraising for terrorist or extremist organizations, people, or supporting groups
  • Expressing support for, condoning, or glorifying mass shootings and other acts of domestic terrorism or violent extremism

2.3 Bullying

Bullying other members using our Services to cause any form of pain, mentally or physically, is strictly prohibited. We have a zero-tolerance policy for threatening others with real-world or online harm, inciting violence against people or property, bullying, stalking, trolling, harassment, intimidation, extortion, and blackmail. This includes:

  • Threatening physical or sexual assault or violence
  • Threatening to harm someone in real life
  • Revealing or threatening to reveal others’ personal information
  • Threatening to take over another person’s account or file false abuse reports
  • Singling out a user or group for ridicule, abuse, humiliation, or degradation
  • Sexual harassment
  • Impersonating individuals, groups, or entities in harmful ways
  • Shaming or stigmatizing body types or physical features

2.4 Suicide or Self-Harm

We take our users’ well-being very seriously. We reserve the right to communicate with authorities regarding credible threats of self-harm. We do not allow content or behaviour that depicts, glorifies, promotes, or encourages suicide, self-injury, eating disorders, or real-world activities that create a serious risk of harm, including:

  • Providing instructions or methods related to suicide or self-harm
  • Content that glorifies, supports, or depicts suicide or self-harm
  • Content that encourages depression, hopelessness, or despair as desirable
  • Discouraging others from seeking help
  • Dangerous physical challenges or stunts in the real world

2.5 Discrimination

DBDCompNet is no place for discrimination and does not condone it. We do not allow discrimination based on:

  • Age
  • Race, perceived race, or ethnicity
  • National origin
  • Sexual orientation
  • Gender, gender identity, or gender expression
  • Religion or religious affiliation or beliefs
  • Disability status including diseases, bodily conditions, disfigurement, mobility issues, and mental impairment
  • Veteran status
  • Caste
  • Familial status

This list is not exhaustive and does not exclude other forms of discrimination that may be enforced.

3. Off-Limits

DBDCompNet is no place for anything outside of Dead by Daylight or its Services.

3.1 Politics

You may not discuss current, past, or future political events. Political discussions can lead to discrimination or controversial conversations.

3.2 Romance and Sexual Content

DBDCompNet is not a dating platform nor a platform to share sexual content. We do not allow:

  • Romantic or flirtatious gestures or communication in a romantic context
  • Pursuing or soliciting romantic relationships online
  • Engaging in unwanted romantic or flirtatious behaviour
  • Engaging in sexually explicit conversations or soliciting material from others
  • Content or behaviour that depicts, implies, or explicitly describes sexual acts
  • Nudity, partial nudity, or other content produced for sexual arousal
  • Sexually suggestive content or behaviour in profile pictures, biographies, statuses, display names, usernames, banners, and emotes

Private or public relationships are to be kept outside of the Services.

3.3 Illegal Goods and Activity

We do not allow discussion, disclosure, sharing, selling, or shopping of illegal goods, nor engaging, implying, or involving others in illegal activity. This includes:

  • Illegal drugs by state law or international standards
  • Pharmaceutical products as well as alcohol, tobacco, vaping, and associated paraphernalia
  • Dietary supplements and enhancers such as weight loss pills and steroids
  • Depiction of intoxicated behaviour associated with consuming alcohol or drugs
  • Bomb, explosive, or weapon-making instructions or schematics in the real world
  • Planning or executing terrorism

3.4 Profanity

We allow limited profanity in the form of swearing, but not insulting. Do not:

  • Try to evade automatically blocked words
  • Upload audio in which blocked profanity is used

Dead by Daylight is a platform intended for ages 18+, though we understand some users may be 16+.

3.5 Violent Content and Gore

Dead by Daylight is a horror game which includes violent scenes such as Moris. However, we do not allow:

  • Violence outside of Dead by Daylight
  • Real-world violence and gore, including actual murder cases
  • Glorifying, disclosing, or sharing animal violence and abuse
  • Violence and torture involving fictional characters outside of Dead by Daylight

4. Integrity

4.1 Cheats and Scams

Advertising, disclosing, sharing, or allowing cheats and other third-party modifications or scams, which are forbidden by law or by Dead by Daylight guidelines, is prohibited. Users are obligated and encouraged to report such behaviour immediately.

Rewards for reports are not provided. Reporting helps keep our platforms and Dead by Daylight safe.

4.2 Spam

We do not allow disrupting or misleading others on our platform. This includes:

  • Mentioning one or several users more than once
  • Mentioning more than 5 users in one message within 10 minutes
  • Repeating messages within a short amount of time or sending similar messages in a different format
  • Creating posts to direct users off the Services

4.3 Intellectual Property

We expect all users to respect intellectual property rights. We will remove infringing content upon receiving a valid copyright or takedown request from the rights holder.

Examples may include:

  • Using another organization’s ruleset on our Services without direct consent
  • Using another artist’s media in your profile or posts without consent
  • Using brand logos or designs without permission

Behaviour Interactive holds rights to the Dead by Daylight logo used in our media. Usage of their media is allowed under their Terms of Use as long as it remains compliant. Upon request, we will take down DBDCompNet or related media, including user creations and content.

4.4 Usage Guidelines

Users and others may use our logo in marketing material if compliant with the Behaviour Interactive Terms of Use.

5. Advertisement

Advertisement for third parties that are not associated with DBDCompNet or its Services is not allowed unless stated otherwise.

You may advertise DBDCompNet members and Services.

6. Security

Your personal safety is extremely important to us. Please take this section seriously and follow it at all times.

6.1 Sharing Personal Information

Users are strictly prohibited from sharing personal information on our Services. Requesting personal information is also forbidden. Personal information includes:

  • Email address
  • Passwords or access tokens
  • Home address or physical location
  • Financial information including credit cards and bank account details
  • Medical information
  • Telephone number
  • Internet identifiers such as usernames or handles not linked to the Services
  • Sensitive credentials such as social security numbers
  • Visuals or audio media of themselves or non-public figures
  • Full legal names

6.2 Directing Users Off-Platform

When using the Services, you may not include links that lead users off-platform.

Allowed links are:

  • Links on the dbdcompnet.com domain, including dbdcompunion.com
  • Links on the bhvr.com domain
  • Links on the deadbydaylight.com domain

7. Misusing Automatic Systems

DBDCompNet may enforce automatic detection systems to detect and moderate misconduct.

7.1 Evading Safety and Security Systems

  • Hiding violating assets
  • Creating or using an account to avoid moderation enforcement
  • Using VPNs to change your location to use the Services despite regional bans
  • Offering access to your account or using another person’s account
  • Using bots to break rules, spam, or crash services, including raiding
  • Screaming or using uncomfortable sounds
  • Falsely reporting users
